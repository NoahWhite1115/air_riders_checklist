Kirby Air Riders

Here's all the checkboxes and their rewards

Complete list of Road Trip checklist missions in Kirby Air Riders

Jump Links

  • Complete Road Trip checklist
  • Road Trip checklist final reward

Kirby Air Riders' Road Trip mode has 150 checkboxes to complete as part of the usual in-game checklist. Each checkbox has a reward locked behind it, whether it's a rider, machine, or some cosmetics to help you deck out your license card.

Notably, some of these checkboxes will unlock content for City Trial, like VS. Boss Stadiums and legendary-machine parts, so make sure to get those done.

You will naturally complete a ton of these missions just from playing the game, but if you're stuck and can't tell what you need to do next, then our list should be able to help you out. You can see the complete list of checkboxes in the Kirby Air Riders Road Trip checklist below.

[Ed. note: Spoilers for Road Trip, including characters and legendary machines are below.]
